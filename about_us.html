Training

We address all kinds of training and certification needs of IT and non-IT technologies. UniversalTech Solutions currently aim to serve students, professionals, educational institutions and corporate clients at a global level. Our training includes latest technologies focused on Power Platform topics including Power Automate, Power Apps, Power BI, Azure, Automation and Microsoft Business Applications. UniversalTech Solutions training provides the industry's most advanced self-paced training courses and prides itself by writing and maintaining hands-on lab exercises that are challenging. Our emphasis on learning through practical application is fundamental in shaping our trainees into being thorough professionals. We encourage the development of a project from every trainee – putting them in touch with their creativity andtaking them through what to expect of a real project development life cycle. This also serves to impress future employers and assures them of the student’s capacity to be independent and self-sufficient.
